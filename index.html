Formulas used
How this works
  • Carb dose = carbs ÷ carb ratio
  • Correction = max(0, (current BG − target BG) ÷ ISF)
  • Total dose = carb dose + correction
  • Est. carb ratio = 500 ÷ TDD
  • Est. ISF = 1800 ÷ TDD (mg/dL per unit)

Correction math is always done in mg/dL internally. mmol/L values are converted using 1 mmol/L = 18 mg/dL.

Safety notes
  • This tool does not replace your clinician or prescribed insulin plan.
  • Sensitivity varies by person, time of day, activity, and illness.
  • Estimated ratios from TDD are rough starting points only.
  • If blood sugar is low — follow your hypoglycemia plan, do not dose from this app.
  • This app does not account for insulin on board (IOB), illness, or exercise.
Privacy

Runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent anywhere. No cookies, no analytics, no storage. Closing the tab clears everything.
