How this works
  • Carb dose = carbs ÷ carb ratio
  • Correction dose = (current BG − target BG) ÷ ISF (never negative)
  • Total dose = carb dose + correction dose
  • Estimated carb ratio = 500 ÷ total daily insulin (TDD)
  • Estimated ISF = 1800 ÷ TDD (mg/dL per unit)

The 500 and 1800 rules are common starting-point formulas for rapid-acting insulin. Correction math is always done in mg/dL internally; mmol/L values are converted using 1 mmol/L = 18 mg/dL.

Safety notes
  • This tool does not replace clinician guidance or your prescribed insulin plan.
  • Insulin sensitivity varies by person, activity, stress, illness, and time of day.
  • Estimated ratios from TDD are rough starting points, not personalized prescriptions.
  • If blood sugar is low, do not dose from this tool — follow your clinician's hypoglycemia plan.
  • Always confirm doses against your care team's written instructions before injecting.
  • This app does not account for IOB (insulin on board), exercise, illness, or other real-world factors.
