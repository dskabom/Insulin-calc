How this works
  • Carb dose = carbs divided by carb ratio
  • Correction = max(0, (current BG minus target BG) divided by ISF)
  • Total dose = carb dose + correction
  • Est. carb ratio = 500 divided by TDD
  • Est. ISF = 1800 divided by TDD in mg/dL per unit

mmol/L values are converted to mg/dL internally using 1 mmol/L = 18 mg/dL.

Safety notes
  • This tool does not replace your clinician or prescribed insulin plan.
  • Sensitivity varies by person, time of day, activity, and illness.
  • Estimated ratios from TDD are rough starting points only.
  • If blood sugar is low, follow your hypoglycemia plan and do not dose from this app.
  • This app does not account for insulin on board, illness, or exercise.
